My Design Principles

Strong conviction

Showing numbers helps to support the design and convince stakeholders therfore I find the data that can support my design. (It can be compeitor's profit numbers, user conversion rate, users reviews/ feedbacks.)

Thoughtful Complexity

I enjoy delving into complex challenges that require deep product thinking. Sometimes, I gather ideas from unrelated products, so I consistently keep an eye on every app or web product I use. Additionally, collaborating with others helps in solving complex problems.

Process Oriented

After becoming a UX/UI designer, I shifted my focus to being more process-oriented. I collaborate closely with my teammates to establish processes that benefit both our teams and users. This involves maintaining flexibility and adaptability to adjust priorities and outcomes as needed.
